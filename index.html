A book called "The fault in our stars"

The Fault in Our Stars by John Green is a heartwarming yet tragic story about Hazel Grace Lancaster, a teenager with cancer, who meets Augustus Waters, a cancer survivor, in a support group. The two form a deep bond, sharing a love for books and philosophical conversations about life, illness, and mortality. As they grow closer, they embark on a journey to meet Hazel's favorite author, which leads to revelations about love, loss, and the fragility of life. The novel explores themes of love, pain, and the search for meaning in difficult circumstances.
